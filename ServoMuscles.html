My Dataset

From that time period, I discovered how much I loved to train and from that point on, I have been training ever since.

One of the major issues I recognized early on and is extremely apparent with the above approaches is that the servos themselves are moving, and so the surface they are mounted to also must allow them to move.

This was a difficult problem to solve, however, I believe I found a good solution which you will see below.
