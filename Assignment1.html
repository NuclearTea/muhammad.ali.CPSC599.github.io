Secret Morse Encoder

What you will need:

  • A computer
  • Arduino or other microcontroller, I use an Arduino Leonardo
  • Serial Terminal, I am using the built in Arduino Serial Terminal
  • Appropriate USB Cable for your microcontroller
  • Rotary Encoder, I am using the KY-040 Rotary Encoder
  • Dual Axis Joystick Module, I am using the KY-023 Joystick Module
  • Breadboard
  • Any Colour LED
  • Resistor
  • 9 F-M Cables
  • 4 M-M Cables
